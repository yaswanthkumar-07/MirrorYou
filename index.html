Your Honest Speaking Mirror — That Teaches

Most tools watch you present and hand you a score. MirrorYou watches, listens, and then gives you a personalized roadmap to get better — exercises, script improvements, and weekly practice plans built around your actual weak spots.

What's Inside

10 Features That Move the Needle

From raw recording to a full improvement roadmap — everything you need in one place.

📷
Webcam Recording

Record presentations directly in your browser. No installs, no friction — just hit record and go.

🎤
Voice Analysis

AWS Transcribe measures your clarity score, words per minute, and detects every filler word.

👁️
Face Analysis

AWS Rekognition tracks eye contact duration and smile frequency throughout your session.

🤚
Gesture Recognition

AI counts and evaluates your hand movements — too few or too many both affect your score.

💡
Confidence Scoring

A composite AI-powered score blending posture, voice, and facial signals into one confidence index.

📊
Dashboard & Charts

Chart.js-powered visual dashboards show your improvement trajectory across every metric over time.

🗂️
History & Reports

Browse past presentations with full feedback breakdowns. Track what changed between sessions.

New 🎯
Action Plan Generator

Every low score becomes a specific drill. Not "improve eye contact" — a timed, repeatable exercise.

New ✍️
Script Improver

Paste your script and Claude/GPT rewrites it for clarity, flow, and audience engagement. Instantly.

New 📅
Weekly Practice Plan

Your personalized 7-day improvement roadmap — built from your specific weak areas, refreshed weekly.
