About the Project

What is MirrorYou?

A full-stack application that turns every presentation into structured, measurable data.

MirrorYou is a full-stack web application built with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js) and integrated with AWS AI services. Users record themselves giving presentations directly in the browser. The system automatically analyzes verbal communication — clarity, pace, and filler words — and non-verbal communication — eye contact, smiles, gestures, and confidence — using AWS Rekognition and AWS Transcribe. All results are saved to MongoDB and displayed on a rich dashboard with historical charts so you can track improvement over time.

Features

What's Being Built

Seven tightly integrated modules covering every dimension of presentation performance.

01
📹

Webcam Recording

Record presentations directly in the browser with no plugins or installs. Video is securely captured and uploaded to AWS S3.

02
🎙️

Voice Analysis

AWS Transcribe drives clarity scoring, words-per-minute measurement, and automatic detection of filler words like "um", "uh", and "like".

03
👁️

Face Analysis

AWS Rekognition tracks eye contact quality and smile frequency frame by frame, surfacing how engaged and expressive you appear.

04
🤲

Gesture Recognition

Counts and categorizes hand movements and body gestures throughout your session to help balance stillness with purposeful motion.

05
🧠

Confidence Scoring

AI aggregates voice tone, posture signals, and facial cues into a single overall confidence score with personalized improvement tips.

06
📊

Dashboard & Charts

Chart.js visualizes every metric across sessions, making week-over-week improvement visible and keeping you motivated to keep practicing.

07
📁

History & Reports

Browse all past sessions, replay recordings, and download detailed PDF feedback reports to share with coaches or track long-term growth.
